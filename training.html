Ticket Types

Your first and foremost responsibility as a mod is to efficiently manage the ticket system in Void Esports. We operate with two primary ticket categories, each requiring specific handling protocols.

General Ticket

These are created by community members for support inquiries, moderator assistance, or reporting issues. Handle with professionalism and follow escalation procedures for complex matters.

Roster Ticket

Application channel for users seeking to join Void Esports. Initial response must include age verification and direction to appropriate resources. Follow structured onboarding workflow.

Protocol: All ticket responses should begin with professional greeting and age inquiry. Direct new applicants to the appropriate channels and maintain detailed records of all interactions.

Mod Responsibilities & Commands

Warn/Report — Issue warnings for rule violations; escalate repeat or severe incidents to senior staff.

LOA — Manage leave-of-absence requests and update roster status accordingly.

Shift Management — Sign in/out for moderation shifts; ensure coverage and handoffs.

Weekly Performance Metrics — Track ticket resolution times, quality of responses, and community feedback.

Ticket Handling Guidelines

All responses must begin with a professional greeting and an age inquiry. Confirm the user's role and provide role-specific instructions. For roster applicants, always ask for age verification and direct them to the onboarding resources. For Grinder+ applicants, follow the Grinder+ instructions exactly. Use the blacklist format for banned users and follow the closing script when resolving tickets.

Interactive Training Phase
